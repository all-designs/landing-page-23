FOR AUTHORS, PUBLISHERS, POETS & RECITERS OF QASIDA

The Fayda Digital app provides free e-books, audiobooks, professional poetry & recitations of titles and tunes published by its parent company (Fayda Books). We also want to give subscribers the opportunity to access other creative works that have been authored or published independently or through other publishing houses. Therefore, an in-app purchase feature of special titles and poetic recitations will be made available at a very affordable cost ($1). Our intention is to bring the beauty and wisdom contained within the Tijani knowledge base to the wider public.
